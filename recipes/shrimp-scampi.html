Shrimp Scampi


This quick and easy shrimp scampi recipe is an Allrecipes fan-favorite, with more than one thousand ratings and hundreds of reviews. And it's ready to serve in only 40 minutes. Ingredients

  • 30 medium shrimp, peeled and deveined
  • 2 tablespoons olive oil
  • 2 cloves garlic, minced
  • 2 tablespoons butter, melted
  • 1/2 teaspoon kosher salt
  • 1/4 teaspoon ground black pepper

Steps

  1. Preheat oven to 350 degrees F (175 degrees C)
  2. Toss shrimp in a bowl with olive oil, butter,. garlic, salt, and pepper
  3. Arrange in a circular pattern in a round casserole dish
  4. Bake in the oven until shrimp turns pink and cooked through, about 15 minutes
